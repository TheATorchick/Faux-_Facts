Other Organizations

Scroll down to learn more about other organizations and what they stand for. You can also click on them to open a link to their sites!

Save A Fox

Save A Fox Rescue is a Minnesota-based nonprofit that rescues and rehabilitates foxes from fur farms, the exotic pet trade, or unsafe situations, providing safe, enriched environments while educating the public about fox care and the realities of the fur industry.

PETA

People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals (PETA) is an international nonprofit advocating for animal rights and opposing the use of animals in fashion, food, and experimentation, including strong campaigns against fur farming.

ASPCA

American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (ASPCA) is a U.S.-based organization focused on preventing animal cruelty through rescue, adoption, veterinary care, and advocacy for stronger protection laws.

Fur Free Alliance

Fur Free Alliance is a worldwide coalition of animal protection groups promoting “fur-free” policies in the fashion industry and raising awareness about fur farming practices.

CAFT

Coalition to Abolish the Fur Trade (CAFT) is an international grassroots group that campaigns against the fur industry through protests, public education, and efforts to reduce consumer demand for fur products.

WWF

World Wildlife Fund (WWF) is a global nonprofit that works to protect endangered species and their habitats. Operating in over 100 countries, it addresses threats like deforestation, climate change, and wildlife trafficking while promoting sustainable practices to reduce harm to animals and ecosystems.
